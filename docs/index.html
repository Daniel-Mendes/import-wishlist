Privacy policy

Profiles features may require to send your numerical Steam ID or GOG ID. To use these features, you may be required to log in inside the extension via Steam and GOG , in which case your numerical Steam ID and GOG ID will be stored on your browser local storage.

To be able to import games to your GOG wishlist, we may store an access-token on your browser local storage.
